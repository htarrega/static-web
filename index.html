Hugo Tárrega

Hi, I'm Hugo! I'm a software engineer and a fiction writer.

I think a lot about human fictions and the power of discourse to shape our structures. Our most fundamental structures—politics, religion, language—are truths we collectively author and sustain, and I love exploring these truths.

My academic background is in computer architecture and software engineering, and my approach centers on storytelling and craftsmanship—in code, literature, and ideas.

I currently work as a software engineer while writing a novel. In it, I try to mix existentialism, immortality, magic, and the legacy of the French Revolution. The narration explores fear of death, what we are capable of doing to avoid it, and grief.

Feel free to roam around this website, which serves as a sampling of what I am currently reading, writing and thinking about. If what you find interests you, feel free to email me.
